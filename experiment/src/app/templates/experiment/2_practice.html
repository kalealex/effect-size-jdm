Remember the Betting Rules

  • The amount you win is proportional to the odds of victory for the team you bet on.
  • If your team loses the round, you lose the amount that you bet.
  • The amount that you do not bet is subject to a flat tax of 25%.
  • Winnings in each round are subject to the tiered tax described in the table.
Tier Tax Rate
0 to 50 cents 10%
50 cents to $1 20%
$1 to $1.50 30%
$1.50 to $2 40%
over $2 50%
